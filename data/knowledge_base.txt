### ORGANIZATION OVERVIEW

NayePankh Foundation is a UP Government registered NGO and one of the biggest student-led organizations in India. The name "NayePankh" means "giving wings" — symbolizing the upliftment of the underprivileged section of society. NayePankh is one of the eminent NGOs espoused in providing food, sanitary napkins, clothes, and educating underprivileged sectors of society for a better future.

### HOW IT STARTED — THE ORIGIN STORY

As we all know, 2020 was a year the world was striving to survive the global pandemic of Covid-19. During these dire times, a group of high school students felt an urge to make an impact with whatever they had. They tried to uplift the underprivileged and help the needy with all their resources and material they could arrange. It was tough but they didn't lose hope and kept going in order to bring a change everyone was expecting the bigger authorities to bring in societies at smaller levels.

They started off as a group of high schoolers but grew into a team of numerous people from different parts of the city and state. On 28th March 2021, they officially landed on the ground to serve their duties as the youth of the most rapidly progressing nation. The main hope of a nation lies in the arms of its youth.

NayePankh Foundation was initiated to bring a change and help people during the tough times of Covid. Later in the year, the NGO started to expand operations and provide help to a wider section of society. With this revamped vision, the NGO acquired the name of NayePankh — giving wings to uplift the underprivileged section of society.

### WHAT THEY DO — PROGRAMS AND SERVICES

1. Food Distribution: NayePankh distributes food not only to underprivileged communities but also to stray animals. They fight hunger actively across their operating cities.

2. Sanitary Napkins and Menstrual Hygiene: NayePankh provides free sanitary pads to underprivileged women. Most young women feel humiliated in public places during their menstrual cycle. To bring change, NayePankh creates awareness campaigns among women and youth about personal hygiene and provides sanitary napkins.

3. Clothes Distribution: NayePankh provides free clothes to poor families across their operating regions.

4. Education: NayePankh educates underprivileged sections of society for a better future. They believe every child has the right to dream and achieve their aspirations irrespective of their socio-economic background.

5. Healthcare Support: NayePankh works to provide access to healthcare and basic amenities to underprivileged communities.

6. Community Empowerment: NayePankh empowers communities to lead better lives and realize their true potential through various programs.

### LEGAL REGISTRATIONS AND CERTIFICATES

NayePankh Foundation holds the following official certifications:

1. UP Government Registered NGO — Officially registered with the Uttar Pradesh Government as a recognized non-governmental organization.

2. 12A Certification — Tax-exempt status under the Indian Income Tax Act. This certifies NayePankh as a legitimate charitable organization.

3. 80G Certification — This means if someone donates to NayePankh, they will get a relief of 50% in income tax. Donations to NayePankh are tax-exempted under 80G of the Indian Income Tax Act.

Official certificates (Society Registration, 12A, and 80G) can be viewed at: https://nayepankh.com/our-certificates
